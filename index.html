VART: Virtual Air Rights Throughput

AI-Powered Satellite Spectrum Optimization

Revolutionizing Satellite Connectivity

VART is BELEOVART's proprietary spectrum optimization engine that maximizes satellite throughput across multiple constellations (Starlink, Amazon Kuiper, Galaxias Space Intelligence, and Chinese LEO operators) through real-time signal analysis, interference prediction, and dynamic frequency allocation.

  • Real-time Signal Analysis: Monitor signal quality across all satellite constellations simultaneously
  • Interference Prediction: ML-powered forecasting with 92% accuracy
  • Dynamic Frequency Allocation: Automatic optimization without spectrum ownership
  • Multi-Constellation Support: Seamless integration with all major LEO operators

VAVX: Virtual Access Vertex Exchange

Decentralized WiFi Hotspot Network Management

Empowering Communities Through WiFi

VAVX is BELEOVART's decentralized WiFi hotspot network platform that enables community members to become connectivity entrepreneurs. Through smart contracts and blockchain-based revenue sharing, VAVX creates economic opportunities while expanding digital access across Africa.

  • Hotspot Ownership: Community members deploy and manage WiFi hotspots
  • Revenue Sharing: 70% to hotspot owners, 10% to VAVX, 20% to platform
  • Smart Contracts: Transparent, automated revenue distribution via blockchain
  • User Engagement: VABOARD advertising platform for monetization

Our Story

BELEOVART was founded on a simple yet powerful vision: Africa deserves sovereign, world-class digital infrastructure that empowers communities and generates economic opportunities. Recognizing the limitations of traditional satellite connectivity and the untapped potential of decentralized networks, we created VART and VAVX—two complementary platforms that together form Africa's most innovative digital infrastructure ecosystem.

VART optimizes satellite spectrum across multiple constellations, delivering 15-30% throughput improvements without requiring spectrum ownership. VAVX enables community entrepreneurs to deploy WiFi hotspots and earn 70% revenue share, creating economic opportunities while expanding digital access.

Together, VART and VAVX represent a new model for African digital infrastructure: sovereign, sustainable, and empowering.
